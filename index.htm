Brainhack Toruń 2019

16-17 November

The Brainhack Toruń 2019 is an official satellite event of the Brainhack Global 2019 (http://www.brainhack.org/global2019/), organized by the students from the Neuroimaging Club at the Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń. We meet together to develop our skills in programming, neuroimaging data analysis, and best practices of reproducible neuroscience. We help each other to make our first steps as brain researchers!
